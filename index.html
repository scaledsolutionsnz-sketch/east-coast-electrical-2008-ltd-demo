What we do

Electrical work for homes and businesses

Whether it’s a flickering light, a dead switchboard or a full rewire on an older Canterbury place, we sort it properly and safely.

Switchboard upgrades

Swap tired old fuse boards for modern RCD-protected switchboards that trip safely instead of overheating.

02

Full & partial rewires

03

About East Coast Electrical

A local sparky who turns up and does it properly

East Coast Electrical (2008) Ltd is a Christchurch electrical business that’s been looking after homes and businesses across Canterbury since 2008. Run by John Lanham, it’s the kind of outfit where the person who quotes the job is the person who does it.

No call-centre runaround, no vague pricing — just honest electrical work, explained in plain English, and left safe and tidy when we go.
