What we do

Domestic and commercial electrical, start to finish.

Whether it's one dead socket or wiring a whole new build, it's the same crew and the same standard every time.

01

Switchboard upgrades

Old ceramic fuse boards swapped for modern RCD-protected boards that cut the power before anyone gets a shock.

02

Rewires & new wiring

Full home rewires and added circuits to current NZ standards — staged room by room so you can keep living in the house.

03

EV charger installation

Home and workplace EV chargers installed and load-checked, so you can charge overnight without overloading the house.

04

Lighting design & install

LED downlights, outdoor and feature lighting planned for the room and fitted neatly — brighter where it matters, softer where it doesn't.

05

Fault finding & repairs

Tripping breakers, dead sockets, flickering lights — tracked down, diagnosed and fixed, usually on the first visit.

06

Commercial & new builds

Fit-outs, maintenance and new-build wiring for Christchurch builders, landlords and businesses — booked around your trading hours.

About East Coast Electrical

Electricians you can actually get hold of.

We've been wiring Christchurch homes and businesses since 2008. We're a small, hands-on outfit — no call centres, no subbing it out to whoever's free. The person who quotes your job is the one who shows up to do it.

From a single dodgy socket to a full rewire or a commercial fit-out, we treat your place like we'd want ours treated: drop sheets down, mess cleaned up, and everything tested and certified before we hand the keys back.
