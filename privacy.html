Privacy Policy

← Back to home

Last updated 14 June 2026. This policy explains how East Coast Electrical (2008) Ltd handles personal information, in line with the Privacy Act 2020. This is a demonstration website; final wording should be confirmed with the business before going live.

What we collect

When you contact us through this website or by phone, we collect the details you give us — typically your name, phone number, email address, suburb and a description of the work you need. That's it.

How we use it

We use your information only to respond to your enquiry, provide a quote, schedule and carry out the work, and send you any related paperwork such as invoices or compliance certificates.

Who we share it with

We don't sell or rent your information. We only share it where it's necessary to do the job — for example with a supplier for materials, or where we're required to by law. Enquiry form submissions are delivered to us by a third-party form service.

How we store it

Your information is kept securely and only for as long as we need it for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law.

Your rights

You can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request a correction at any time. Just get in touch.

Contact

Any privacy questions? Call us on 0800 477 275.
