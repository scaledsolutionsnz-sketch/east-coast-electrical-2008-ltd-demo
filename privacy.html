Privacy Policy

How we handle the details you share with us. Last updated 2026.

The short version

We only collect the information we need to respond to your enquiry and do the job. We don’t sell it, and we don’t share it with anyone who isn’t helping us run the business.

What we collect

When you fill in a form on this site, we collect the details you give us — typically your name, phone number, email, suburb and a description of the work you need. That’s it.

How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry and prepare a quote
  • To carry out and manage any work you book with us
  • To keep in touch about your job

How it’s handled

Form submissions on this site are delivered to us by email through a third-party form service. Your details are stored and used in line with this policy and the Privacy Act 2020.

Sharing

We don’t sell or rent your information. We’ll only share it where it’s needed to do the work (for example a supplier or sub-contractor on your job) or where the law requires it.

Your choices

You can ask to see the personal information we hold about you, correct it, or ask us to delete it. Just get in touch and we’ll sort it.

Contact

Any privacy questions? Call us on 0800 477 275.
